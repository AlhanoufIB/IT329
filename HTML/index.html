Crescent Table

Where recipes meet tradition.

Crescent Table is a recipe-sharing platform designed especially for Ramadan. It brings together traditional and modern dishes in one place, making it easier for families and individuals to plan their Iftar and Suhoor meals.


What You Can Do

  • Browse recipes shared by other users
  • View recipes by category such as main dishes, desserts, and appetizers
  • Like and favourite recipes you enjoy
  • Create and manage your own recipe collection
